# SFC Exam Requirements — RA 1 / RA 4 / RA 9

Source: SFC Guidelines on Competence (October 2024 version). All references in this file point to that document.

This is the **authoritative** reference for the agent. Do not invent alternative paper combinations or exemption logic. If a candidate's situation is not covered here, mark the exam position as **TBC** rather than guess.

## 4. Exemption (Exemption) pathways

### 4.1 RIQ exemptions

| Exemption type | Applies to | Conditions |
|---|---|---|
| **Full RIQ exemption** | RO / LR | (i) Currently or within the past 3 years held a licence with equivalent RIQ requirements **and** applying for the same role (e.g. RA 1 LR → RA 4 LR); **or** (ii) applying for a temporary licence to carry on RA 1, 2, 4, 5, 6, 10 or 11. *(SFC Guidelines on Competence Oct 2024, §4.4.2.1)* |
| **Conditional RIQ exemption** | RO / LR | Currently licensed; has 5 years of local relevant experience within the past 8 years; applies for an RA with **different** RIQ requirements but **same** role; must complete additional 5 hours CPT (industry/product knowledge); SFC may impose business-scope limits. *(§4.4.2.2)* |
| **Re-entrant RIQ exemption** | RO / LR | Re-applying after 3–8 years off-register; same RIQ + same LRP + same role; must complete CPT (5 hours per RA per year out, round up; ≥50% on local regulatory knowledge). *(§4.4.4.1)* |

### 4.2 LRP exemptions

| Exemption type | Applies to | Conditions |
|---|---|---|
| **Full LRP exemption** | RO / LR | Any of: (i) currently or within past 3 years held a licence with equivalent LRP requirements + same role; (ii) applying for temporary licence (RA 1, 2, 4, 5, 6, 10 or 11); (iii) full-time HK regulatory/compliance work — ≥3 years within past 6 years on the same RA scope (SFC may impose limits). *(§4.4.3.1)* |
| **Conditional LRP exemption 1** | RO only | Senior overseas hire lacking local regulatory experience. Threshold: (i) ≥8 years relevant experience in an FRR Schedule 3 jurisdiction; **or** (ii) ≥6 years relevant experience with ≥2 years HK-licensed; some experience in past 3 years required. Must be supervised by a designated RO; business-scope limits; +5 hours CPT (local regulatory knowledge). *(§4.4.3.2)* |
| **Conditional LRP exemption 2** | RO only | Currently licensed RO with 5 years HK relevant experience in past 8 years applying for an RA with **different** LRP requirements; +5 hours CPT; SFC may impose limits. *(§4.4.3.3)* |
| **Conditional LRP exemption 3** | LR upgrading to RO | LR upgrading to RO on the **same RA**; must additionally hold ≥3 years recent HK-licensed relevant industry experience; +5 hours CPT. *(§4.4.3.4)* |
| **Conditional LRP exemption 4** | RO only | ≥8 years relevant experience in an FRR Schedule 3 jurisdiction; sponsoring corporation serves Professional Investors only as a fund manager (or its group), or for RA 9 manages private investment funds; +5 hours CPT. *(§4.4.3.5)* |
| **Conditional LRP exemption 5** | LR only | Itinerant Professionals: ≤30 days per calendar year of RA work in HK, accompanied by a licensee — or alternatively serve only Professional Investors. Sponsoring corporation retains full supervisory responsibility. *(§4.4.3.6)* |
| **Conditional LRP exemption 6** | LR only | Currently or within past 3 years was an LR; **and** (i) never sat HKSI LE Paper 1 and applies for an RA with same LRP + same role; **or** (ii) applies for an RA with **different** LRP + same role; +5 hours CPT. *(§4.4.3.7)* |

## 5. How to read a candidate's licence record into a paper position

Use this decision logic. Only conclude **satisfied** when one of the conditions below is clearly met. Otherwise mark **likely gap** or **TBC**.

### Step A — Has the candidate ever passed each required paper?

A paper is **demonstrably passed (or exempted)** if **any** of the following is true:

| Evidence | Implies |
|---|---|
| Currently or previously held an LR or RO licence in the same RA | All papers required for that LR / RO are met |
| Currently holds an LR licence and applies to upgrade to RO on the same RA | Paper 1 and Paper 7 + RA-specific RIQ are met by virtue of the LR; for RA 1/4 LRP the LR only has Paper 1 — RO additionally needs Paper 2; for RA 9 LRP the LR only has Paper 1 — RO additionally needs Paper 6 |
| Holds a different RA's LR / RO with overlapping papers | Only the overlapping papers are met (Paper 1 always overlaps; Paper 7 overlaps across RA 1/4/9) |
| Off-register 0–3 years | All papers previously held remain valid; full LRP exemption available (§4.4.3.1(i)) |
| Off-register 3–8 years | All papers previously held remain valid under the re-entrant exemption (§4.4.4.1) — **no re-exam**; only CPT top-up required (5 hours per RA per year out, ≥50% local regulatory) |
| Off-register >8 years | Re-entrant exemption no longer available — full re-examination assumed unless another exemption applies |

### Step B — What is still missing?

- For RA 4 + RA 9 RO: required set is {1, 2, 6, 7, 8, 12}
- Subtract the demonstrably-passed set from the required set
- The remainder is what the candidate **still needs to pass**

### Step C — Is the right exemption available?

- If there are missing papers, check exemption table (§4) for any path that would substitute.
- A common Brooklet path: LR-to-RO upgrade on same RA → §4.4.3.4 (LRP exemption 3). Requires ≥3 years recent HK-licensed experience + 5 hours CPT.

### Step D — State the conclusion in **one sentence** (per current Brooklet style)

Format:
> "Combined [RA list] [LR/RO] requires N papers: Paper [...]. From [evidence], the candidate has demonstrably passed Paper [...] (or holds equivalent exemption). [Still needs to pass Paper X / Within 3–8 yr re-entry window — no re-exam, only CPT top-up of N hours]."

## 6. Common traps (do not fall into these)

1. **A long licensing history does not by itself satisfy the new RA's papers.** Cross-check overlap explicitly.
2. **LR ≠ RO papers.** RA 1/4/9 LR has Paper 1 only as LRP; RO additionally needs Paper 2 (RA 1/4) or Paper 6 (RA 9).
3. **Type 9 LR alone does not prove Type 9 RO.** Holding T9 LR proves Paper 1, 7, 12. T9 RO additionally needs Paper 6. Always state Paper 6 as outstanding unless evidence shows otherwise.
4. **Re-entrant 3–8 yr window means no exam, but does mean CPT.** State both halves.
5. **CPT hours under §4.4.4.1 are per-RA per-year-out.** A 17-month gap on one RA = round up to 2 years × 5 hours = 10 hours per RA, ≥50% local.
6. **Exam status of a *historical* RO is normally proven** — they had to pass to get the RO. Use this when reading older RO records.
